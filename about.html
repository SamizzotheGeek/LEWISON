WHO WE ARE

Lewison Builders and Contractors Limited is a user trade name for Building and Construction which is a Kenyan based consultancy firm registered under Lewison Group Limited for structural, civil, mechanical, electrical, environmental, water and sanitation engineering.
Lewison Builders and Contractors was establishes in the year 2010, registered under Lewison Group Limited with their main offices in Nakuru County, Naivasha Sub-county and in Nairobi.
The firm has been operating for several years and has earned a reputation for innovation, quality and cost effectiveness in projects ranging from high rise office and residential buildings, commercial development, public infrastructure development among other infrastructure.

The scope of engagement encompasses feasibility studies & concept development, preliminary & detailed design, project planning & management, contract documentation and tendering, construction supervision and contract administration. We utilize our experience, capabilities, resources and diversity of competence developed through a wide spectrum of projects successfully undertaken to give our clients and consultants their most desired results.
The company has a core team of staff who are comitted to meeting the client's needs and are well equipped with plant machinery as well as automated management.

Our mission

To provide quality, innovative and efficient work, helping our clients to minimize construction risks and maximize construction opportunities in a multicultural business environment at competitive prices. We are well equipped in terms of plant, machinery as well as integrated management and delivering outstanding results in a record time of all our assignments.

Our Vision

Our vision is to turn plots into homes and architectural aspiration into reality, much faster and cost effectively, making housing affordable even to the low and middle income earners here in Kenya and the entire African Continent.

Our Objectives

Our objective is to provide quality services to our clients efficiently, and to their satisfaction. In order to ensure this, we have a team of qualified and experienced staff who are committed to meeting the needs of our clients.
