General Building and Construction

We are the premier custom builder in Kenya. Our history in the country leaves a track record for integrity and professionalism. Our quality is uncompromised.
We offer construction services from the inception to completion of your project. We are involved in every stage of the project from architectural design, plan approval and building. This holistic approach delivers cost effectiveness, streamlines project management and shortens construction timelines.
Each client is different so are your needs. We ensure that our team of architects custom-design a unique and appealing home that fits your budget.
We help in plan approvals with the county government to ensure that there are no delays. Our construction managers are in charge to ensure that the highest standard of construction are adhered to.
